Networking App

This social networking app connects you with people nearby who have similar responses (at least 75%) on surveys.

Users take surveys based on pop culture entertainment, comparing fictional characters presented as gifs using the Giphy API.

After completing a survey, results are compared to other users and matched. Matches are then shown their relative locations using the Google Maps API.

Networking apps often focus on shared interests so we decided to try something different, comparing users' dislikes instead.

Take a look: Networking App

Google Maps marks your relative location and default radius. The bigger the pink circles inside that radius, the more users are in those relative locations.

A list of surveys to choose from

A Match has been chosen, Google Maps shows your approximate distance and opens a chat between you.

User Interviews

Discussing the issue with interviewees helps to reveal what actual problems exist and how they feel about the topic. Managers should avoid assumptions about what Users actually want beforehand.

Define Personas

User Interviews will also elucidate who your real Users are, defining them in Personas helps to organize your target audience.

Experience

Structured, unbiased questions in User Interviews will lead to users actual experiences and how you can realistically improve upon it. You may find they don‚Äôt need improvement, in which case your preliminary research saved you the effort of developing unnecessary products.
