Project HateDate

HateDate is a social networking app, connecting you with people nearby based on shared hates rather than likes.

Users take surveys based on pop culture entertainment such as The Simpsons, Star Wars and Star Trek by comparing characters through gifs using the Giphy API.

After completing a survey, their results would be compared to other users and if a strong match (greater than 75%) was found they'd be connected, showing their relative locations using Google Maps API.

Take a look:
https://hatedate.herokuapp.com/

Google Maps marks your relative location and default radius. The bigger the pink circles inside that radius, the more users are in those relative locations.
